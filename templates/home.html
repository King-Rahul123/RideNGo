About RideNGo

RideNGo is a multi-agency vehicle booking platform designed to simplify vehicle rentals for customers while giving full control to individual travel agencies.

The system provides a unified digital environment where multiple agencies can register, log in, and manage their own fleet

of vehicles, pricing, and bookings. At the same time, users can easily search, filter, and book vehicles based on their needs such as tours, weddings, meetings, and other travel purposes.
